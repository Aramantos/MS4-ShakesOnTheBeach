About Us

Shakes on the Beach is a sea side cafe offering an assortment of food and drinks.

Our supremely picturesque location offers a dining experience that is not to be missed. Our services have proven a delight to both customers and critiques alike. Enjoy a delicious meal and rest from your food coma on our decking for as long as you wish. T&C's apply

  • Music Nights every Tuesday
  • Kids 50% off on Saturdays

Our Story

Our cafe sprang up from a couple of locals who felt they could fill a need in the market. There are a lot of dining establishments in the area but not we feel that can off the service we provide, and our customers agree.

We began as a burger and shake shack but have been growing the business as our means grew. Our roots are in this community and they seem to grow deeper and deeper with every deep dish pizza that we send out.

We hope you join us soon for the first of many visits to our establishment.
